Video Player

Video modules have one of the best reusable features of the app whilst still maintaing a new experience per car With the flexibitlity to display anything that can be rendered into a video in the app the user can be transported straight into the action with the RAV4 or zipping through the streets of the city in an Aygo. Or maybe you just wish to show a 3D representation of how much storage spac is avaliable in the boot when the seats are down, there is a wide breadth of content opportunities to exploit.

When creating experiences for the car the app has two prebuilt solutions for handling video. An option to display auto playing full screen video with audio and also the option to launch into another module using a video. See the options below for examples of what can be achieved.
